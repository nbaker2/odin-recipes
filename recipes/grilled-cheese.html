Grilled Cheese Sandwich

Description

What other to pair your tomato soup with than a hearty grilled cheese sandwich! Including the butter, this grilled cheese used four types of cheeses to achieve maximum gooiness and taste. Enjoy!

Ingredients

  • Sourdough bread
  • Sliced smoked provolone cheese
  • Sliced white cheddar cheese
  • Sliced havarti cheese
  • 4 tablespoons butter, unsalted (you will have some leftover)
  • 1-2 cloves minced garlic
  • about 1/4 cup shredded mild cheddar cheese

Steps

  1. Take your butter, garlic, and shredded cheese, and mix together in a bowl. Set aside.
  2. Spread some of your butter across both slices of sourdough.
  3. Add the cheeses on top of one of the slices of bread, buttered side down (in this order): 2 slices of provolone, 1 slice of white cheddar, 1 slice of havarti. If you prefer more gooiness, use 2 slices of cheddar. Top with the second slice of bread, buttered side up.
  4. Heat a skillet over medium to high heat. Once heated, add your sandwich to the skillet.
  5. When the bread is nice and crusty, flip (will be about 3-4 minutes depending on how hot the skillet is). Do the same for the other side.
  6. Serve immediately.
